Directgate connects your devices over private, encrypted links. It removes the need for complex networking tools like VPNs or port forwarding. You gain remote access to your computer from anywhere while your data stays private. The software uses peer-to-peer technology to build a direct path between your machines.

## 📋 What this tool does

Directgate handles your remote connections. It uses high-grade encryption to ensure only you see your data. 

* **Secure Remote Access:** Open a terminal or file window on your home computer from your office.
* **Safe Transfers:** Move files between computers without sending them through a cloud server.
* **Terminal Access:** Run commands or manage server settings as if you sat in front of the machine.
* **Peer-to-Peer:** Your data travels directly from one computer to the other.
* **No Open Ports:** You do not need to change firewall settings or open ports on your router. 
* **Zero-Trust Security:** Every connection requires strict proof of identity.

Once you finish the installation, you must link your computer to your account. 

1. Launch the Directgate application from your Start Menu.
2. Create an account if you do not have one. 
3. The app generates a unique ID for your machine.
4. Repeat these steps on your second computer. 
5. Enter the unique ID from your home machine into the Directgate app on your remote machine.
6. The connection will start automatically.

## 🛡️ Understanding the security

Directgate protects your privacy through current security standards. 

* **AES-256 Encryption:** This standard scrambles your data. Nobody can read the files or commands you send.
* **End-to-End Encryption:** Your data stays encrypted from the moment it leaves your device until it reaches the destination.
* **SRP-6a:** This protects your password during the sign-in process. Your actual password never travels across the network.
* **NAT Traversal:** This technique allows the software to find a path through your router without manual configuration.

## 🛠️ Frequently asked questions

**Do I need a static IP address?**
No. Directgate finds your computers automatically even if your home internet provider changes your IP address.

**Does this software store my files on the internet?**
No. Files stay on your computers. Directgate only creates the connection between them.

**Can I run this on a work computer?**
Most systems allow this, but check with your IT department if your company has strict rules about remote access software.

**What happens if I lose my internet connection?**
The software will pause your session. Once your signal returns, it will try to reconnect your machines.

**Is this safe for home use?**
Yes. Peer-to-peer connections are often safer than cloud-based alternatives because your data does not sit on a third-party server.

## ⚙️ Managing your devices

The main screen lists every computer you linked to your account. You can rename devices to make them easier to find. Use the settings menu to toggle features like file sharing or terminal access. If you get a new computer, you can remove the old device from the list to keep your account clean. 

The software monitors the health of your connection in the status bar. A green light means your connection is stable and encrypted. A yellow light means the software is searching for a path between your computers. A red light means the connection is offline.

## 📁 Troubleshooting tips

If you struggle to connect, check these items:

* **Check the Internet:** Ensure both machines have an active web connection.
* **Restart the App:** Close the program fully and open it again.
* **Check for Updates:** Older versions might lose the ability to sync. Update the software if a button appears in the settings menu.
* **Firewall settings:** In rare cases, Windows Firewall may ask for permission to let Directgate talk to the network. Select "Allow" when the pop-up appears.

Directgate simplifies the way you reach your files and terminal tools. By removing the need for port forwarding and VPNs, it keeps your network settings simple and your data stays on your hardware. Keep the app updated to ensure you have the latest security improvements.
